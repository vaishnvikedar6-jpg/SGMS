Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Data Collection

The Student Grievance Management System (SGMS) collects essential academic data including Student Name, PRN, and Institutional Email to facilitate the grievance resolution process.

2. Data Usage

Information collected is strictly used for departmental tracking and administrative responses. We do not share personal data with third-party advertising entities.

3. Security Framework

Access to the Command Center and Faculty Node is restricted via multi-layer authentication to ensure institutional integrity and student confidentiality.
