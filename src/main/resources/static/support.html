Support Center 🌟

👑 How long for a resolution?

Most grievances are addressed within 24 to 48 working hours by the department heads.

✨ Can I edit a filed complaint?

Once submitted, a complaint is locked for review. You can add "Follow-up notes" in the History tab.

🔐 Is my identity private?

Yes, all grievances are encrypted and only visible to the assigned administrative officer.
